Theory
1: What is NLP?
Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that enables computers to understand, process, analyze, and generate human language. It allows people to communicate with computers using natural languages such as English, Malayalam, Hindi, and Tamil. 

2: Why do we need NLP?
NLP is needed because humans communicate using words and sentences, while computers understand only binary data (0s and 1s). NLP acts as a bridge between humans and computers, making communication easier and more natural. 

3: Explain three NLP applications.
1.	Chatbots – Chatbots like ChatGPT answer user questions and provide information. 
2.	Language Translation – Applications such as Google Translate convert text from one language to another. 
3.	Sentiment Analysis – Companies analyze customer reviews to determine whether opinions are positive, negative, or neutral. 

4: Difference between NLP and Computer Vision.
NLP: Works with text and language, Used in chatbots and translation, Processes human language.
Computer Vision: Works with images and videos, Used in object detection and face recognition, Processes visual information.

5: What is text data?
Text data is information represented as words, sentences, or paragraphs. Examples include emails, messages, social media posts, customer reviews, and articles. NLP mainly works with text data.

4: Explain how ChatGPT uses NLP.
ChatGPT uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand the user's questions and generate meaningful responses. It analyzes the words, sentence structure, and context of the input, then predicts and produces a relevant answer in natural language. This enables smooth and human-like conversations.
